BatBall
Classic bat-and-ball gameplay with 3D bricks, glowing effects, power-ups, and a simple white paddle like your example.
Move Use Left / Right arrow keys or move your mouse.
Goal Break all the bricks to clear the level.
Power-ups Catch WIDE, SLOW, and MULTI power-ups.
Rule If the ball falls once, you lose immediately.
Game Over
The ball escaped.
