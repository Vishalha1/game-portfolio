Snake

Control a glowing rainbow snake, eat apples, and survive as the speed gets faster.

How to Play

  • Arrow Keys to move the snake
  • S to stop or resume the game
  • R to reset and restart
  • Each apple gives you 1 point
  • Every 10 apples, the snake gets faster
  • Do not hit the wall or your own body
You can also press Enter to start
